Clinical summary: This meta-analysis suggests that the clinical intervention is not clearly effective after one week. Effect noted by Fukusaki at one week from corticosteroids is similar to the effect of local anesthetics reported in their trial and the trial of Cuckler. However, the quality of evidence is low due to imprecision of results and the heterogeneity or inconsistency of results. Additional interpretations may be in the references in the 'Cited by' section below.

We included trials of epidural injections of corticosteroids as add-on therapy to usual care.

Methods overview:   Studies were abstacted into standardized tables of , and results. This repository updates a previously published meta-analysis.(1) Newer studies included are listed in the references. Rationale for newer trials excluded may be listed at the end of the references. See methods for details.
